## WHY
BST gives ordered ops O(log n) balanced; inorder sorted. Validate, kth, range queries. Sorted insert degrades to list.

## THEORY
Left<root<right; inorder ascending.
```mermaid

 A[root]-->B[<left]
 A-->C[>right]
```
