## WHY
Matrix-chain, burst balloons need choosing split points; brute force exponential. Interval DP O(n^3) over lengths.

## THEORY
dp[i][j]=min over k of dp[i][k]+dp[k][j]+cost.
```mermaid
flowchart TD
 A[len]-->B[try k]
 B-->C[combine]
```
